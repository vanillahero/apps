How to Play

Goal: Beat 9 AI opponents in a 10-race championship to get the highest score!

Controls:

  • W: Accelerate
  • S: Brake/Reverse
  • A/D: Steer
  • Spacebar: Drift
  • Shift: Use Boost
  • P: Pause

On the Track:

  • Hold Spacebar while turning to drift. Drifting charges up your boost meter!
  • When the boost meter is full, press Shift for a powerful speed increase.
  • Driving on the grass will slow you down.
  • - Pick up lightning bolts for an instant speed boost!
  • - Avoid oil slicks, they'll make you spin out!
